HIVE TERMS & CONDITIONS

1. Acceptance of Terms

By using HIVE, users agree to comply with the system rules and academic policies.

2. User Accounts

Users must provide accurate account information including email, profile picture, and credentials.

3. Academic Purpose Only

HIVE is intended solely for group participation tracking, task monitoring, and academic collaboration.

4. Participation Records

Users must not falsify contribution records, participation logs, or task activities.

5. Teacher Validation

Teachers may validate participation and review contribution reports to ensure fairness.

6. Data Privacy

<> HIVE follows the principles of the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012

The system may collect:

  • Email Address
  • Profile Picture
  • Participation Logs
  • Task Records

7. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for maintaining account security and ethical use of the system.
