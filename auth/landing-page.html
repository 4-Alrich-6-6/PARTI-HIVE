WHAT IS THE SYSTEM ABOUT

HIVE is a web-based Group Participation Tracking System that promotes fairness, accountability, and transparency by allowing users to record, review, and validate each member’s contributions.

With HIVE, every effort counts.

WHAT CAN THE SYSTEM DO

Track Participation

Leaders can record and monitor each member's participation.

Manage Tasks

Create and assign tasks to members easily.

View Reports

Students can monitor their contribution records.

Teacher Validation

Teachers can review participation records fairly.
